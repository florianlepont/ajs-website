## Files Created/Modified

## Decisions Made
- Footer Instagram link placed in its own `<nav class="footer-social-nav" aria-label="Social">`, appended after `.footer-legal-nav` rather than merged into it — preserves the legal nav's existing DOM order/tests and matches the UI-SPEC's explicit instruction.
- `.contact-page__social` is a distinct class from `.contact-page__lead` (identical CSS declarations) per the plan's explicit "do not reuse the lead class name" instruction, to keep "form intro" and "alternate contact channel" semantically separate.
- Footer's new-tab hint is a frontmatter const (`instagramNewTabHint`); About/Contact hints are literal inline `.sr-only` spans — matching each file's own pre-existing convention for how copy is authored (frontmatter consts in `BaseLayout.astro` vs. hardcoded template strings on content pages).
