## Phase Goal

**As a** site visitor, **I want to** browse an Éditions overview and open a full per-édition detail page in French or English, **so that** I can discover Romane's self-published paper éditions as a pure showcase (no pricing or checkout).

*(This plan is the production-hardening slice: it makes the shipped Éditions pages discoverable in the sitemap (ROADMAP success criterion 5) and turns EDN-06's "zero commerce affordance" boundary into a build-blocking automated guard.)*

<objective>
Close the two cross-cutting requirements the overview/detail slices leave open: (1) add the Éditions overview and per-édition detail URLs to `sitemap.xml` for both locales (ROADMAP success criterion 5), and (2) convert EDN-06's "no pricing/availability/purchase affordance" from a visual check into a build-blocking negative assertion by extending the existing `verify-static-artifact.mjs` CI guard to scan the rendered Éditions HTML for forbidden commerce strings.

Purpose: Sitemap inclusion makes the feature discoverable by search engines; the build guard guarantees the no-commerce boundary survives future edits, which no existing gate protects.
Output: An extended sitemap endpoint, extended sitemap unit/e2e assertions, and a build-blocking commerce guard.
</objective>
