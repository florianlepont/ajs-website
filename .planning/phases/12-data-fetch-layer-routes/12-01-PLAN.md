## Phase Goal

**As a** site visitor, **I want to** browse an Éditions overview and open a full per-édition detail page in French or English, **so that** I can discover Romane's self-published paper éditions as a pure showcase (no pricing or checkout).

*(This plan delivers the first vertical slice of that goal: the data-fetch layer + the overview listing. The detail page is Plan 12-02; sitemap + commerce-boundary hardening is Plan 12-03.)*

<objective>
Ship the Éditions overview as a complete vertical slice: add the build-time `edition` data-fetch layer to `src/lib/sanity.ts` (mirroring the `gallery` pattern, with the two research-confirmed deviations — simpler publication filter, no `seo` field), then build the FR + EN overview pages as a vertical editorial zigzag list (D-01 through D-04). After this plan a visitor can open `/editions/` or `/en/editions/` and see every published édition by title, lead photo, and full statement, each row linking to its (not-yet-built) detail page, with zero commerce affordances.

Purpose: The overview is the entry point to the Éditions feature and the first thing a visitor sees; the shared data-fetch layer it introduces is what Plans 12-02 and 12-03 build on.
Output: `Edition`/`EditionImage` types, `getEditions()`/`getEdition()`, two overview route files, one new unit test file, and the overview block of a new e2e spec.
</objective>
