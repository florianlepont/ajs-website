**As a** site visitor, **I want to** browse an Éditions overview and open a full per-édition detail page in French or English, **so that** I can discover Romane's self-published paper éditions as a pure showcase (no pricing or checkout).

*(This plan delivers the second vertical slice: the per-édition detail page. It builds on Plan 12-01's data-fetch layer and overview links.)*

<objective>
Ship the per-édition detail page as a vertical slice that mirrors `galleries/[slug].astro` with the three CONTEXT-locked deviations: a clickable hero (D-05), the combined `[leadPhoto, ...images]` Lightbox array with inverted index math (D-06/D-07), a compact format-details line (D-09/D-10), and an in-flow "Retour aux éditions" back-link (D-08). Purpose: The detail page is where a visitor actually views an édition's photo shoot and format details — it completes the browse→open journey the overview started.
Output: Two detail route files and the detail/lightbox/no-commerce blocks of the e2e spec.
</objective>
