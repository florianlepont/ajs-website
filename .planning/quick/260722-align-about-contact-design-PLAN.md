# Align About and Contact design

## Goal

Make the About and Contact pages feel like two parts of the same editorial
system while preserving their distinct content structures.

## Decisions

- Use Contact's restrained type scale as the shared reference.
- Share a 1180px content frame, 0.8/1.2 desktop proportion, 40–56px display
  title, 18–22px lead, label styling, top rule, and responsive spacing.
- Keep About's numbered editorial sections and Contact's functional form card.
- Apply the same contracts to French and English pages.
- Add a browser test comparing computed typography and container geometry.
